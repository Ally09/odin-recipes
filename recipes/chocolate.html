Three ingredients vegan chocolate bar

Simple to make!

You need to try these three ingredient chocolate bars! They are very easy to make and are quite tasty treat!

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup. coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup. cocoa powder
  • 2-3 tbsp maple syrup or honey

Steps:

  1. Mix all your ingredients into a bowl making sure it is mixed well with no lumps.
  2. pour finished mixed ingredients into molds and put into the fridge wait an hour for it to firm up.
