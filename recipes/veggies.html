Steamed veggies

Quick healthy veggie dish!

Healthy side dish of steamed veggies! Nothing is more simple then a fresh and crunchy steamed vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • Broccoli
  • Asparagus
  • Green beans
  • Cauliflower

Steps:

  1. Fill the base of a two tier steamer pan (2inches) of water.
  2. Place the first steam basket on the top of the pan of water and place the carrots in the steamer basket.
    Place lid on the pan turn heat down to medium, once the bottom pan brings to a boil.
  3. Once cooked remove veggies from the steam basket and place in a warm serving bowl.
  4. Top with your choice of sauce, and enjoy.
