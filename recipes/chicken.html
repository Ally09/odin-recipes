Chicken Wrap

Simple to make chicken wrap!

Are you looking for an easy dinner or snack to make that won't break the bank? Look no further! Whole grain wrap filled with chopped chicken fresh tomatoes, whole grain brown rice, and parmesan chesses.

Ingredients:

  • Cooked chopped rotisserie chicken
  • 2 tbsp. graded Parmesan cheese
  • 4 oz. chopped cherry tomatoes
  • 4. (10') whole wheat wraps
  • half a cup. cooked brown rice
  • 6 tbsp. cilantro sauce

Steps:

  1. Add your graded parmesan cheese, chopped tomatoes, cilantro sauce
    and brown rice into a bowl stir all into the bowl to your liking.
  2. put all your ingredients into the whole wheat wrap. Fold in sides of the wrap, then tightly roll bottom
    away from you, keeping the sides tucked into until a burrito.
