Garlic cilantro lime rice

Try this easy side dish!

Easy tasty side dish that will go with anything! Prep time is about 5 mins.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups. long grain rice
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • Juice of 3 limes and zest of 2 limes
  • Chopped fresh cilantro, for garnish

Steps:

  1. Cook over a low heat for 3 minutes, stirring constantly to make sure
    the rice doesn't burn. Add 2 cups of the juice and zest of the limes and bring to a boil.
    Reduce the heat to low, cover and simmer for 10 to 15 minutes or until the rice is done.
  2. Just before serving, stir through the juice of 1 lime and lots of chopped cilantro.
